Orange Tofu

Description

This is an wasy to make receipe that is perfect for friday night dinners or a quick but yummy and nourishing lunch on the go.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup veggetable oil for frying
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1 package firm tofu
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup orange juice
  • 1/4 warm water
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp cornstarch
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 carrots, sliced

Directions

  1. Heat 1/4 cup oil in a wok over medium-high heat. Place the 1/4 cup cornstarch in a dish; press tofu slices in the cornstarch to coat on all sides. Stir-fry in the wok 5 minutes, or until golden brown on all sides. Drain tofu on paper towels. Allow wok to cool, and wipe clean.
